About SAIStival

SAIStival brings together Africa’s leading Agritech founders, investors, and ecosystem builders in a setting designed for collaboration and growth.

Over 2.5 days, participants step out of the usual conference format into a curated experience blending learning, connection, and action. Expect immersive field visits, expert-led workshops, and interactive activities that spark new ideas and unlock practical solutions.

More than networking, SAIStival builds trust, shares knowledge, and creates partnerships to scale Africa’s most promising Agritech ventures.

What to Expect

  • Peer learning sessions shaped by founder-driven topics
  • Expert-led workshops on fundraising, talent, and growth
  • Curated networking with investors
  • Immersive experiences to build trust and collaboration
