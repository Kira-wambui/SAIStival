About SAIStival

SAIStival brings together Africa’s leading Agritech founders, investors, and ecosystem builders in a setting designed for collaboration and growth.

Over 2.5 days, participants will step out of the usual conference format into a curated experience blending learning, connection, and action. Expect field visits, expert-led workshops, and interactive community activities that spark new ideas and unlock practical solutions.
