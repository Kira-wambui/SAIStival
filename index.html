About SAIStival

SAIStival brings together Africa’s leading Agritech founders, investors, and ecosystem builders in a setting designed for collaboration and growth.

Over 2.5 days, participants will step out of the usual conference format into a curated experience that blends learning, connection, and action. The program weaves together immersive field visits, expert-led workshops, and interactive community activities that spark new ideas and unlock practical solutions.

More than just networking, SAIStival is about building trust, sharing knowledge, and creating the partnerships needed to scale Africa’s most promising Agritech ventures.

FAQ

Who is SAIStival for?

SAIStival is designed for the current SAIS cohort of founders, with invitations also extended to a select group of active alumni. A limited number of ecosystem partners and funders will also be present.

Can alumni or additional team members attend?

Yes. Active alumni and additional team members can join for a contribution of $400, which covers participation in the full 2.5-day program and access to discounted ATS tickets. Please note that SAIS only covers travel and accommodation for one representative per startup.

What makes SAIStival different from a regular conference?

SAIStival blends the focus of a conference with the energy of a festival. Instead of sitting in hotel ballrooms, you’ll take part in field visits, interactive workshops, and shared experiences in unique settings. The agenda is designed to be structured, authentic, and engaging, with every activity focused on building real connections and practical outcomes.

What kinds of sessions will take place?

  • Peer learning sessions shaped by founder input
  • Expert-led workshops on key topics like fundraising, talent, and growth
  • Curated networking opportunities with investors
  • Immersive community experiences that foster collaboration and trust

Will SAIS provide travel and accommodation?

Yes. SAIS will cover flights and accommodation for one representative per startup in the current cohort. Hotels will be booked for the full week (From Saturday to Friday) for all non-Nairobi-based founders.

How do I register?

Eligible participants will receive an official invitation with registration details.

Who will be speaking at SAIStival?

Speakers will include leading founders, funders, and ecosystem builders from across Africa and beyond. The lineup will be announced closer to the event.
